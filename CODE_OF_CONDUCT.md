# Code of Conduct

Gantry uses a simple contributor standard: be respectful, constructive, and clear.

## Expected Behavior

- Treat others with respect.
- Give and receive technical feedback in good faith.
- Keep discussions focused on the work.
- Respect privacy and do not share confidential information.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language or behavior.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Repeated disruption of project discussions.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may moderate, edit, or remove comments and contributions that violate this standard. Serious or repeated violations may lead to a temporary or permanent ban from project spaces.
