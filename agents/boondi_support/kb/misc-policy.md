# Boondi Misc Policy KB

Do not put live opt-out state, current job openings, franchise policy, or
partnership truth here unless confirmed by BSS source data.

## Routing Rules

### Spam

- Do not spend tools or customer-facing energy on obvious spam.
- If runtime requires a reply, keep it minimal and do not invite more back and
forth.

### Opt-Out And Promotional Messages

- If an opt-out capability/source exists, use it before confirming action.
- If no action capability exists, use the fixed safe shape: acknowledge, say
opt-out status cannot be confirmed here, and say this chat's contact details
will be passed to the team for review.
- Do not open a skill or call a lookup tool for basic opt-out/unsubscribe/spam
requests; this fixed safe shape is sufficient unless a real approved opt-out
action capability exists.
- For repeated opt-out complaints, use the same fixed safe shape plus brief
empathy.
- Do not claim "you have been unsubscribed", "removed from our list", "you
won't hear from us", actioned, flagged, messages will stop, or equivalent
completion unless the system confirms it. Also avoid "action it" wording; say
the team can review the opt-out request. Do not ask for phone/WhatsApp/email
again.

### Franchise And Jobs

- Share franchise or careers information only from confirmed policy/source.
- If confirmed policy says no franchise, decline politely and thank them.
- If a jobs/careers route exists, share it concisely; otherwise capture role
and location and route.
- Do not invent website, store-visit, email, careers-link, application,
franchise-fee, partnership-process, or hiring-status guidance.

## MCP Boundaries

- No MCP needed for obvious spam.
- Use an opt-out/source capability only if it exists and is approved for the
agent.
- Do not use Shopify or CRM customer-order tools for franchise/jobs/spam.
- Use source data for current careers link/process or franchise policy.

## Handoff Brief

When routing to team, pass only facts already known:

- opt-out text and repeated-complaint signal
- phone/context from verified conversation
- franchise/partnership ask
- job role/location if volunteered
- exact customer ask

## Reply Checks

- No false action confirmation.
- No invented careers link, franchise policy, or partnership process.
- No internal words such as MCP, KB, source adapter, or trace.
